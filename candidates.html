How SkillScanr Works for Candidates

A simple process when you apply to companies that use SkillScanr for transparent, fair hiring

1

Apply to a SkillScanr Company

When you apply to a company using SkillScanr, upload your CV and we'll do the heavy lifting. We'll scan your experience, extract your skills, and create your skills profile to generate a tailored assessment automatically.

2

Complete Your Assessment

Complete a skills-based assessment generated from your CV. The assessment focuses on the skills and experience you already have, ensuring an accessible format that lets every candidate demonstrate their abilities fairly.

3

Get Clear Feedback

Receive transparent feedback on your performance. No ghosting, no guesswork. Just honest insights into your strengths and growth areas.

4

Explore Role Matches

Based on your assessment, discover other roles within the company that match your skills. Get personalized learning recommendations to help you grow toward your ideal position.
